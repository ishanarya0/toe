Tosh Kheerganga Kalga

The three perfect locations to camp, trek and travel in parvati valley. Alongside Tosh village is the Tosh River, which begins at the Tosh Glacier within Tosh Valley, which is a feeder valley for the Parvati Valley. Kheerganga perfect challenge for all adventure seekers, its a 6km trek from barsheni dam. The top has mulitple dooms and camps for stay and an amazing natural hot spring to replensish after a long trek. Kalga is yet another smnall village near the barsheni dam and pulga village, the place is untouched and families are really welcoming. It is best to enjoy some quiet time with these small connected families in their home.
