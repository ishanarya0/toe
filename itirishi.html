Rishikesh

Located in the northern state of Uttarakhand in Dehradun district of India, Rishikesh is a small town situated alongside the Ganges river concluding down the Himalayas. Known for its scenic beauty and adventure-filled attributes, Rishikesh is a perfect place for a weekend getaway.

It is also known as “World Capital of Yoga” featuring numerous yoga retreats and ayurvedic treatment centers. Apart from that, it is only 230 km from Delhi to Rishikesh which makes it even more preferable by the tourists all across the country.

Rishikesh is the beginning point for Chaar Dhaam Yatra (four shrines pilgrimage tour) and is considered to be one of the holiest cities in India, which gives it the authentic touch of Indian religious culture.

White water rafting, rock-climbing, cliff jumping, kayaking, trekking to nearby places, bungee jumping, and various other activities serve as a cherry on the top of the cake making it a complete vacation spot for those looking for a short refreshing holiday.
